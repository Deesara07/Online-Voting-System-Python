# Online-Voting-System-Python
Using this python package people can make online voting for their election

The system manages multiple political parties and their candidates, each with various attributes such as name, NIC, age, education, and state/province. Citizens are allowed to vote for up to three candidates in order of preference, provided they are above 18 years of age. Candidates are associated with a specific state/province and citizens can vote only once within their region. Design a user-friendly system that facilitates citizen voting, ensures candidate eligibility, and tabulates vote counts.
